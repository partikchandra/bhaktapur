DATTATREYA TEMPLE
Binod Rajupadhyaya

He has drawn a map in the style of Vaastu diagrams and while it related to fairly accurate places, it is represented as halfway between a map and a diagram. He has drawn the Jagganath Temple in Bhaktapur and the complex as would have existed in the past. Places which no longer exist have been drawn by him as ‘lost’ buildings of Bhaktapur that he feels need to brought back. So in a sense this is an aspirational diagram instead of a representational diagram.
