Lakshmi Prasad Rajchal

(Male, 45, deceased in 2020) was a contractor who had extensive experience working on conservation projects in the Valley. He described how the labour from Bhaktapur was a valued asset throughout the region, and crucial to ongoing reconstruction.
DATTATREYA TEMPLE

Lakshmi Prasad Rajchal drew the roof plans of different buildings in the Durbar Square and the Taumadhi Square. He labelled the Faasi Dega, Nyatapola, Bhairav Temples but he has also drawn the roofs of Chyasilin Mandap, the Vatsala Devi Temple, the big bell platform and the Pashupati Nath temple. He had notionally drawn the profiles of the Palace.
