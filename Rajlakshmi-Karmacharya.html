He has mapped a sense of boundary and a sense of personal navigation through the city. For her, the boundaries of the city end at her own tole, the Hanuman Ghat (next to the river) and possibly the highway to the north. She begins her map as a route from the Taumadhi to the rest of the city and the paths are indicative of connecting destinations, so more diagrammatic. She has not mapped any specific buildings except for the Mahakali Temple which is her family temple.
