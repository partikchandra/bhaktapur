Indra Kaji Shilpakar

Indra Kaji Shilpakar (Male, 70) is master wood craftsman who has worked extensively across the Kathmandu Valley. He is passionate about the craft and has a workshop in his home in Khauma Tole, which he designed and constructed incorporating old and new influences.
