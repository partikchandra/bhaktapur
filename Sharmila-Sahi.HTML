Sharmila Sahi

(Female, aged 40) works with her husband in their butcher shop. She is originally from Kathmandu and moved to Bhaktapur after her marriage approximately 24 years ago. She observes that the hygiene in Bhaktapur and the dust have improved significantly and caste-based discrimination has decreased, though not disappeared. She prefers to roam around outside the city
