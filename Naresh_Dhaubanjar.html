Naresh Dhaubhanjar

(Male, aged 42) is a guesthouse owner. He was born and spent his early childhood days in Sukuldhoka, after which he moved to the Durbar Square. He has moved out to Katunje for more space but spends a lot of time near the Durbar Square.
NYATAPOLA TEMPLE

Naresh drew an abstract version of the city, with three specific landmark features. The ponds on the periphery of the city, the hills in the background and the tiered temples within possibly three squares of the city though these are more diagrammatic. The boundary drawn is abstract apart from its connection to the ponds and hills.
