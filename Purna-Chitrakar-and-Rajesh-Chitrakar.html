Purna Chitrakar and Rajesh Chitrakar

Purna Chitrakar (Male, aged 73) and Rajesh Chitrakar (Male, aged 43 ) are both artists who specialise in preparing Navadurga masks in Bhaktapur. They play an important role in various festivities in the city. Both agree that Bhaktapur has changed significantly in the past couple of decades, from education to buildings and lifestyles
