Punyeshwari Lage

Punyeshwari Lage (Female, aged 50) is a construction worker and farmer who was born in Byaasi but has since moved to Golmadhi. She only began to work in reconstruction after the Gorkha Earthquake, along with other women in Bhaktapur.
