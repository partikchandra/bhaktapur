Binod Raj Sharma Rajupadhyaya

(Male, 65) is a Sanatan Hindu priest, who lives in Taumadhi. He was born and brought up in Bhaktapur and has spent a lot of time in the Durbar Square.

LEGENDS

Extracted information from interviews mapped as three layers-

: Memory, Childhood, Residence, Neighbourhood.
: Locations and buildings cited as important for daily/ weekly/ritual activity relating to self.
: Locations and buildings cited as important for tourism/ heritage/ Bhaktapur/ relating to others

Binod Rajupadhyaya has drawn a map in the style of Vaastu diagrams and while it related to fairly accurate places, it is represented as halfway between a map and a diagram. He has drawn the Jagganath Temple in Bhaktapur and the complex as would have existed in the past. Places which no longer exist have been drawn by him as ‘lost’ buildings of Bhaktapur that he feels need to brought back. So in a sense this is an aspirational diagram instead of a representational diagram.
