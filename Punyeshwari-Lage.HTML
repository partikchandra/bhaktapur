Punyeshwari Lage

(Female, aged 50) is a construction worker and farmer who was born in Byaasi but has since moved to Golmadhi. She only began to work in reconstruction after the Gorkha Earthquake, along with other women in Bhaktapur.

LEGENDS

: Memory, Childhood, Residence, Neighbourhood.
: Locations and buildings cited as important for daily/ weekly/ritual activity relating to self.
: Locations and buildings cited as important for tourism/ heritage/ Bhaktapur/ relating to others
TAUMADHI

Punyeshwari Lage drew her current place of work, along with the assistance of several bystanders- which is the Faasi Dega Temple. She has presented a notional understanding of work, prayer and the pathways that connect the two as a mind-map tole- Kolachen.
