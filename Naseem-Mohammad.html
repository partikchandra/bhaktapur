Naseem Mohammad

(Male, aged 65), owns a shop in Itachchen which is where he was born as well. For him the masjid is one of the main places in the city that is important to him. He keeps busy and doesn’t explore the city much. He thinks the city has changed, lifestyles and buildings have all changed

Sohev grandson of Naseem Mohammad, drew the map as he gave directions. He has drawn landmarks beyond the city limit, though he did draw the Masjid (mosque) from Bhaktapur describing it as a holy’stupa’ of the Muslims. He has drawn the Nyatapola as a temple from Bhaktapur and the Siddha pokhari pond which is within the city.
